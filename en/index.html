How to learn English by playing this game ?

This site will allow you to work on your vocabulary. It is not about learning grammar or conjugation. The objective of this game is to allow you to familiarize yourself with the vocabulary of a particular language and then discover the correspondence of this word in other languages.

The context given by the category will guide you. The search for correspondence between the letters displayed and the possible words in the language of your choice will help you to develop a reflection that makes sense in that language.

Have fun and enjoy the discovery.
